What is Robotic Engineering

Robotic engineers use computer aided design and drafting(CADD) and computer-aided manufacturing (CAM) systems to perform their tasks . Robotics engineers use computer-aided design and drafting (CADD) and computer-aided manufacturing (CAM) systems to perform their tasks. Robotics research engineers design robotic systems and research methods to manufacture them economically. In short terms they create robots for dangerous tasks where no other human can do.

Education

The Robotic Engineering requirement are having a bachelor's degree in Mechanical Engineering or related fields and having a state license.Hands-on experience is an integral part of becoming a robotics engineer. These professionals work mainly with mechanical machines in various technological departments. Licensing is required by state for those wishing to work in a higher-level position while those entering on a beginner's level only need a bachelor's degree.
